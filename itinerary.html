🎒 Monsoon Packing List

Essential items for July monsoon travel

👕 Clothing

  • 3-4 quick-dry t-shirts (NOT cotton)
  • 2-3 trekking pants / travel pants
  • 1 light jacket / fleece (Mussoorie evenings)
  • 1 waterproof jacket with hood
  • 6-7 pairs socks (extra for monsoon)
  • Nice outfit for full moon dinner (Day 6)
  • Nice outfit for celebration dinner (Day 4)
  • Comfortable PJs (2 sets)

👟 Footwear

  • Waterproof hiking boots (ESSENTIAL!)
  • Sandals for hotel
  • Water shoes for Robber's Cave

🌧️ Monsoon Gear

  • Large umbrella (compact + sturdy)
  • Waterproof poncho (backup)
  • Waterproof phone pouch
  • Waterproof bag covers / dry bags
  • Silica gel packets (for electronics)

💊 First Aid & Medicines

  • Electral/ORS sachets (10 packets)
  • Norflox TZ (diarrhea - 10 tablets)
  • Paracetamol (fever/pain)
  • Antifungal powder (feet!)
  • Band-aids & antiseptic
  • Motion sickness pills (mountain roads)
  • Insect repellent (DEET 30%+)
  • Hand sanitizer

📱 Electronics

  • Phone + charger
  • Power bank (20,000mAh)
  • Camera + extra batteries
  • Universal adapter
  • Headphones
  • Laptop + charger (for work days)

📄 Documents

  • ID proof (Aadhaar / Passport)
  • Travel insurance (MANDATORY!)
  • Hotel booking confirmations
  • Emergency contacts (printed)
  • Prescription copies

🎒 Other Essentials

  • Reusable water bottle
  • Sunglasses
  • Sunscreen SPF 50+
  • Small backpack (day trips)
  • Journal + pen
  • Book for travel
  • Plastic bags (for wet clothes)

💊 Common Monsoon Issues

Diarrhea/Food Poisoning Take Norflox TZ + Electral. If severe (blood/fever): Hospital immediately.
Dengue Symptoms High fever + body pain + headache. Go to hospital for blood test (platelet count critical).
Hypothermia (Mussoorie) Uncontrollable shivering + confusion = danger. Get dry + warm + seek help immediately.

🌧️ Monsoon Safety

Landslide Risk Check road status daily: Call 1070
Never travel during very heavy rain.
If Stuck in Landslide Stay in vehicle. Call 1070 or 1090. Wait for clearance (2-6 hours typical).
Flash Flood Warning Never cross flooded streams. Water is stronger than it looks. Move to higher ground immediately.
